About Us

TrackMais is the leader in Automotive IoT. Our mission is to increase the efficiency, safety, and sustainability of the operations that power our economy.

TrackMais portfolio of complete Internet of Things (“IoT”) solutions combine hardware, software, and cloud to bring real-time visibility, analytics, and AI to operations. We serve customers across diverse sizes and industries, from transportation and logistics to field services, food production, energy, construction, local governments, and manufacturing.
